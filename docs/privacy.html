Privacy Policy for AniniTools

Last Updated: January 7, 2025
Effective Date: January 7, 2025

Introduction

AniniTools ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Concert Mode Messages: Custom text messages you create are stored locally on your device only
  • App Preferences: Settings like brightness levels, strobe frequencies, and color choices are stored locally
  • Favorites: Saved message templates are stored locally on your device

Automatically Collected Information

Device Sensors (Required for App Functionality)

We access the following device sensors to provide app features:

  • Camera/Flashlight: To control LED flashlight and strobe functions
  • Microphone: To detect music beats for beat-sync strobe mode (optional, only when feature is used)
  • Location (GPS): To display coordinates in compass and calculate altitude (optional, only when features are used)
  • Motion Sensors: Accelerometer, gyroscope, and magnetometer for compass, spirit level, and metal detector tools
  • Proximity Sensor: To detect nearby objects
  • Light Sensor: To measure ambient light levels
  • Barometer: To calculate altitude
  • Battery Status: To display battery level in sensor dashboard

Analytics & Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to:

  • Understand app usage patterns (e.g., which features are most used)
  • Identify and fix crashes and bugs
  • Improve app performance and stability
Firebase collects:
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Anonymized usage statistics (feature usage frequency)
  • Crash logs (if app crashes)
  • General location (country/region level only, not precise location)

Advertising

We may use Google AdMob to display advertisements:

AdMob may collect:
  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for general location-based ads)
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions)
Your choices:
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Ad-free version may be offered as an in-app purchase option
  • All ad data collection is governed by Google's AdMob policies

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide App Features: Enable flashlight, strobe, sensors, and tool functionality
  • Improve User Experience: Understand which features are popular and need improvement
  • Fix Bugs: Identify and resolve crashes and technical issues
  • Optimize Performance: Ensure the app runs smoothly on different devices

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All user-created content (messages, favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device using secure storage
  • We do not transmit your personal messages or settings to any server
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

Cloud Storage

  • Firebase Analytics data is stored on Google's secure servers
  • Crash reports are stored securely and are anonymized
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Firebase (Google): For analytics and crash reporting (covered by Google's privacy policy)
  • AdMob (Google): For serving advertisements
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government regulation

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we collect
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Disable Firebase Analytics in your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Analytics)
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable camera, microphone, or location access in device settings at any time

Children's Privacy

AniniTools does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
