Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does the app need camera permission?

A: The camera permission is required to control your device's LED flashlight and provide strobe/concert mode features.

Q: Does the metal detector really work?

A: Yes! It uses your device's built-in magnetometer sensor to detect magnetic fields from metal objects. Calibrate it first for best results.

Q: Is my data being collected?

A: All your settings and data are stored locally on your device. We only collect anonymous analytics to improve the app. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Why is the compass not accurate?

A: Try calibrating your device by moving it in a figure-8 pattern. Also ensure you're away from metal objects and magnetic interference.

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Yes! All features work completely offline. Internet is only used for optional analytics.

Q: How do I report a bug or request a feature?

A: Please email us at aniniwtf@gmail.com with details about the issue or feature request.
